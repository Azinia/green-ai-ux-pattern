Make AI Not Just Smart—But Aware

Empowering Sustainable AI with One Simple UX Pattern

A simple interface that shows users the energy, time, and carbon cost of their AI tasks—before they run. This empowers platforms and developers to build responsible, sustainable AI experiences.

What It Does

  • Shows estimated GPU time
  • Displays electricity use (kWh)
  • Estimates carbon emissions (CO₂)
  • Gives users options: [Proceed] [Eco Mode] [Cancel]

Why It Matters

  • Millions of AI queries run every day—often invisibly
  • No feedback = unconscious, wasteful behavior
  • Users want smarter, greener choices
  • Platforms save energy and align with ESG goals

Green Impact at Scale

Scenario: 1 million image generations per day, each using ~0.01149 kWh

If 30% switch to Eco Mode (40% savings):

  • ~1,379 kWh saved per day
  • ~41,370 kWh saved per month
  • ~210 metric tons of CO₂ saved per year

That’s equivalent to:

  • Removing 45+ gasoline cars from the road
  • Powering 150 homes for a month
  • Saving over 500,000 smartphone charges
